Welcome to AR Café

Founded in 2015, AR Café began as a neighborhood spot for friends and families to gather over coffee and conversation. Our mission is to serve handcrafted drinks and freshly baked treats in a space that feels like home.

We started with a simple vision: create a place where the community could come together to share stories, relax, and enjoy high-quality food and drinks. Our team is passionate about sourcing local ingredients, supporting nearby farmers, and creating a menu that reflects the heart of our town.

From the beginning, we’ve believed in more than just coffee. We believe in creating meaningful connections—between people, between cultures, and between ideas. Whether you're stopping in for your morning espresso or spending a cozy afternoon working on your laptop, you'll find a welcoming environment and friendly faces ready to greet you.

At AR Café, we're proud to host open mic nights, book clubs, and art showcases, all of which highlight the creativity and spirit of our local community. We invite you to be part of our story—because every cup we serve is brewed with care, warmth, and a smile.
